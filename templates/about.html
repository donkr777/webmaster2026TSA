About This Website

This website was created for the Technology Student Association (TSA) Webmaster competition. The purpose of this site is to educate users about the Lenape Native American tribe and their historical presence in Pennsylvania, while aligning with the national TSA theme.

Purpose and Theme

The goal of this project is to preserve and share historical knowledge using modern web technologies. By combining history with technology, this website demonstrates how digital tools can be used to keep cultural stories and traditions accessible for future generations.

Target Audience

This website is designed for students, educators, and community members interested in Pennsylvania history and Native American culture. The content is presented in a clear and organized way so it can be easily understood by a wide audience.

Design and Development

The website was developed using HTML, CSS, and Python with the Flask framework. Visual elements such as maps and historical images were included to enhance learning and engagement. The layout focuses on readability, accessibility, and ease of navigation.

Development Process

The project began with research and planning, followed by wireframing and content organization. Pages were built incrementally, tested for functionality, and refined to ensure accuracy and consistency with TSA competition guidelines.
